Wellfare in India: Empowerment or Dependency?
Join the Discussion Live

Hi there,

MahaSamvaad is back  with bang!

In this episode, we dive deep into one of the most debated questions of our time — is welfare in India a tool for empowerment or a crutch for dependency?

Join us for a  thought-provoking discussion that explores the many layers of India’s welfare ecosystem — from social safety nets and policy reforms to cultural perceptions and real-world impact.  
This is your space to listen, question, and contribute to a meaningful national dialogue.

Key Pointers

Is welfare genuinely empowering citizens, or is it being misused as a political or personal crutch? Explore real-world scenarios and differing perspectives.

Are welfare/freebies schemes equitable, or do they unintentionally favor certain groups? Let’s dissect how policy frameworks shape dependency and empowerment.

How do welfare schemes interact with India’s plural legal and cultural systems? What reforms could make them more empowering? and much more!
